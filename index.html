Eli

Ilya Minin (Eli) is a multimedia artist specializing in interactive projects and generative transformations, as well as sound design and research into acoustic phenomena. His education spans several fields, including art, restoration, music, and sound design, giving him a diverse range of skills and knowledge.

Ilya's portfolio showcases his extensive collection of work, which includes over 56 albums, 37 hours of recordings, and various EPs, LPs, and singles. One of his earlier projects, Micen, combines artistic declamation with a minimalistic electronic sound, showcasing his ability to merge different artistic styles and genres.
