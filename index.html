Some Background on the Artist

Ilya Minin (Eli) - soundartist, researcher of acoustic phenomena; eccentric artist working with computer graphics, video art and granular synthesis.

Works in the gallerave genre of elements of electroacoustics, dance music, algorithmic formulas, etc.

The basis of the method of work is the use of specially selected and tuned natural magnetic forces.
