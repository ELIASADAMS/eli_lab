Artist Statement

Ilya Minin, also known as Eli, is a visionary artist who combines CGI, sound, and interactive media to create captivating art.

His work blends classical traditions with cutting-edge technology, and has been showcased at festivals and galleries across Europe.

Eli's art knows no boundaries as he pushes the limits of traditional and contemporary art techniques.
