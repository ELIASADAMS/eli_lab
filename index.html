Artist Statement

In a world where boundaries blur, technology and tradition intertwine, a visionary artist like no other has emerged. Ilya Minin, known as Eli, captivates hearts and minds with a symphony of CGI, sound and interactive media.

His creations are more than mere artistry, a fusion of classical traditions and the cutting edge of technological innovation. With every stroke of genius, every note of musical brilliance, Eli weaves a tapestry that embraces both the past and the future.

From major festivals to intimate galleries, his work has captivated audiences across the European landscape. Enter a realm where installations soar to towering heights and live performances dance with intimate intricacy. Eli's artistry knows no boundaries as he shapes traditional elements with avant-garde techniques.

But it is his dark sense of humor, his unique perspective on the contemporary tapestry of media trends, that sets his creations apart from the rest. In the realm of Eli's mind, laughter is intertwined with reflection, and irony dances with introspection. With each piece, he invites us to explore the depths of how we think and feel.

For those seeking an aural and visual experience like no other, Eli's masterpieces are the gateway to a realm of innovation and eclecticism. His creative brilliance shines like a beacon, guiding us on a journey where tradition and technology coexist harmoniously.

Prepare to be enchanted. Prepare to unveil the beauty hidden within the imaginative mind of Ilya Minin, a true master of his craft.
