Eli

About Me: I am a contemporary artist, working in: soundart, videoart, digital design and monumental painting. I took a course at the Simon Fraser Institute on Acoustic Ecology with Professor Barry Truax. Now I am studying at the Vibration Laboratory with Andrey Smirnov (Theremin Center), performing multi-channel audio-visual concerts in galleries such as GRUND Solyanka, Khodynka and Polytechnic Open Foundations.

What I have: Diploma in music and art schools, a couple of musical awards; I own musical instruments such as: keyboards, brass, modular synthesizers. I have studio equipment, two musical projects, from which I have released a couple of dozen albums and participated in four compilations. I carry out orders connected with 3D-modeling, generative sound, scoring of exhibition halls, decorating of sound installations and video-art.

Programs, I can also work with: Adobe (PS, AI, AE, AP), FCPX, Zirkonium, Izotope RX, SoundHack

Audio programming languages: Csound, MAX MSP, Pure Data

Knowledge of Languages: Russian (native language), English (Advanced), French (Pre-intermediate)
